PARAMETERS MENU

Wavelet Type



  • This menu item sets the wavelet type and the sampling in the CWT domain.  A choice of 4 different wavelets are available.  The Morlet wavelet usually gives a CWT that can be interpreted using Fourier concepts where scale is directly related to Fourier period, in seconds.  The Morlet wavelet also minimizes the effects of seismogram discontinuities.
  • Wavelet choice shows up in the upper right box of the GUI.
  • The Morlet wavelet is the default choice.
