BCseis Help Pages
Version 1.1
"BCseis", short for "Block Choice seismic analysis", is an interactive GUI to read a single seismogram in SAC format and to perform a variety of analyses using the Continuous Wavelet Transform (CWT) of the signal.

Acknowledgements
The utility to read SAC files was derived from Sandia Lab's MatSeis software primarily written by Mark Harris.

Forward and inverse CWTs are performed using functions available from the Synchrosqueezing Toolbox V1.21 by Eugene Brevdo and Gaurav Thakur.  (https://github.com/ebrevdo/synchrosqueezing).

The use of this material is gratefully acknowledged by the authors.
